Lifelong Companion

Leather is unique.

Antithesis of mainstream fashion, each piece is carefully designed and handcrafted, that only gets better with age. Our vegetable tanned leather has distinct character of its own, is custom-developed using the finest quality tanneries from local Indonesian sources.

We see our product as piece of art, made for your lifelong companion.
